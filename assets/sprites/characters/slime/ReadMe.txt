Stuff to know - Slime animations

- Has 4 states: 
	Idle:
		- has 6 frames divided into 16x16 pixels
		- only has 3 directions facing down, left, right
	Move:
		- has 6 frames divided into 16x16 pixels
		- only has 2 directions facing left, right
	Hit: 
		- has 3 frames divided into 16x16 pixels
		- only has 3 directions facing down, left, right
	Attack:
		- has 6 frames divided into 32x32 pixels
		- only has 2 directions facing left, right
